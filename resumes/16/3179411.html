        Amy has over 10 years of work experience in Greater China. Amy has extensive experience in corporate strategy, operations, product management through her experiences at a top tier US management consulting firm and a plethora of Internet companies in China. Amy has worked in B2C consumer retail industry in China, both as an entrepreneur and senior management for leading Chinese online retailers, which gave her a strong background in operations, sales & marketing, new BU building, negotiations, and Chinese consumer insights. Currently, Amy is focused on helping early stage start ups in China to grow into successful companies through her role as AVP at NetEase Capital, the VC arm of NetEase, Inc.

        Amy graduated cum laude from Wharton School and University of Pennsylvania with BA and BS degrees. Majored in finance and international studies.
